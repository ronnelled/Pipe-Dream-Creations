Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some common questions about our handmade products and custom orders.

Do you accept custom orders?

Yes. We accept custom orders based on color preferences, themes, occasions, and design ideas.

How long does it take to complete an order?

Completion time depends on the size and complexity of the order. Small orders may be completed quickly, while larger or custom requests may take longer.

Do you make products for events?

Yes. We create handmade decorations and pieces for birthdays, parties, celebrations, and other events.

Can I request specific colors or themes?

Absolutely. We welcome customization requests and do our best to match your preferred style.

How do I place an order?

You can contact us by email or social media with your request, and we will respond with next steps.
